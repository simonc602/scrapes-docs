---
title: "Connect a workspace to Team OS"
description: "Connect a local Agentic OS workspace to a hosted Team OS server."
---

Use this section to connect a local Agentic OS workspace to a hosted Team OS
server.

You need:

* the Team OS server URL;
* an active server account;
* your email and password;
* access to a Team before creating Team work.

A Company Admin can sign in without access to a Team. The connection remains
valid, but Team work stays unavailable until a Company grant or direct Team
role gives access.

Team Members also need the client and skill grants required for their work.
Users with Full access do not need separate client or skill grants.

The saved local profile belongs to the server and signed-in user. It can list
and select multiple Teams without creating a separate login for each Team.

If the server is not deployed yet, start with
[Host Team OS](/docs/deploy/team-os-hosted-server).
