---
title: "VPS"
description: "Deploy Team OS on a VPS."
---

On a VPS, run the same two services:

* Postgres with `pgvector/pgvector:pg16` and a persistent volume.
* `memory-api` behind a TLS reverse proxy such as Caddy or nginx.

Keep Postgres private.

If you need local database access for maintenance, use SSH, WireGuard, or a
firewall rule. Do not open Postgres to the internet.
